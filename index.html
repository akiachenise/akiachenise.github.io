About Me Get to Know me.
I'm a creative Front-End Software Developer specializing in Web Development
I'm extremely passionate about Web Accessibility and I believe the Web should be accessible to everyone. Web Accessibility is not a luxury, it is a human right. Aside from my passions behind accessibilty, I am also passionate about creating beautiful works of art through front end design. I have been developing websites since highschool, where I got my start at making web pages for Myspace for my friends. My love for programming grew and I have not looked back. I absolutely enjoy problem solving and helping others. I feel like the best programmers are people who give back to the dev community. I really enjoy teaching children how to program. I had a blast learning and teaching young girls in grade sixth through eigth grade how to code at Black Girls Code , as well as working with school aged children during Apple's Code Camp as an Instructor.
